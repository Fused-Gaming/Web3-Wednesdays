## Executive Summary

This research identifies 15+ notable local and regional tech/Web3 events for Web3 Wednesdays to consider for partnerships, sponsorships, and curriculum integration. The Bay Area hosts a robust conference ecosystem spanning gaming (GDC), AI (NVIDIA GTC), blockchain development (ETHGlobal, Funding the Commons), and startup ecosystems (Startup Grind). Strategic alignment opportunities exist with security-focused tracks, gaming economics, and developer onboarding programs.
